tomcat.moahub.org:

The website is powered by Tomcat and Guacamole. Tomcat is an open-source web server and servlet container. Guacamole is an open-source remote desktop gateway. Tomcat and Guacamole are licensed under the Apache License 2.0. For more information about Tomcat and Guacamole, and their licensing, please visit The offical Apache Website.

phoneinfoga.moahub.org

PhoneInfoga is an open-source tool designed to gather information about phone numbers. Using PhoneInfoga to collect information about someone without their permission might be against the law in some places. It's really important to understand and follow the rules before using this tool. PhoneInfoga is provided under the GNU General Public License (GPL) v3.0, which means it's free to use, change, and share, but you also have to share any changes you make to it. You can find the official PhoneInfoga website and instructions at https://sundowndev.github.io/phoneinfoga/.

ntfy is an open-source and self-hosted push notification server. It lets you send notifications to various devices and platforms, including smartphones, desktops, and browsers. You can use it for personal projects or integrate it into applications for sending alerts, reminders, or any other kind of real-time updates. ntfy is licensed under the MIT License, which is a permissive open-source license. This means you can use, modify, and distribute ntfy for free, for both commercial and non-commercial purposes. You are responsible for complying with any applicable laws and regulations related to your use of ntfy. Website: https://github.com/binwiederhier/ntfy Documentation: https://ntfy.sh/docs Community forum: https://ntfy.sh/docs ntfy requires some technical knowledge to set up and use. You will need to host ntfy on your own server or cloud platform.

uptimekuma.moahub.org

Uptime Kuma is an open-source monitoring tool you can host yourself to keep track of the availability of websites, APIs, and other online services. It checks their status at regular intervals and alerts you if they go down.
